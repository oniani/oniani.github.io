Privacy for the Web
Fri Jan 3, 2020 · 143 words · 1 min

A few words concerning the privacy on the web.

NOTE: The blogpost will get occasional updates, when needed.

Here are my recommendations for the web privacy.

  • Lightweight Privacy: Emphasis on Usability and Speed

    • Brave as the default browser

      • Bitwarden for password management
      • Searx as the default search engine
      • uBlock Origin to block ads (and other junk)

    • Signal as a messenger

    • Element for group chats and collaborations

    • Jitsi Meet in lieu of Zoom

    • Tox is also great for group (or one-on-one) chats

    • Protonmail for the email service

  • Heavier Privacy: Emphasis on Privacy

    • Use Tor
  • Super Advanced Privacy: Stronger Emphasis on Privacy
    • Do not use the web
